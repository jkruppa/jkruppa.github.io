Shiny Apps

The following Shiny apps include bioinformatic teaching topics.

  • Geneset enrichment app A Shiny app to introduce the geneset enrichment analysis.

  • Vulcano plot app A Shiny app to show the effects of the Type I error and the fold change on the vulcano plot.

  • Normalization app Visualization of different normalization methods for microarray data.

  • Prinicple component and clustering heatmap app Visualisation of the principle component analysis and the effects on a cluster heatmap depending on the foldchanges between three treatment groups.

  • Clustering app Visualization of different clustering methods depending on the sample size of the predefined groups.
