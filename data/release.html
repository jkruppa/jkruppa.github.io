AnAge Build 15: Release Notes

Release date: July 3, 2023
Number of entries: 4,671

Build 15 represents a major improvement of AnAge. Over 400 new species were added, and hundreds of longevity records and entries were updated with data from over 150 new references. As previously, the release of a new build means that HAGRID numbers were reshuffled.

Please remember that these notes refer to the latest stable build of AnAge. The current AnAge stats may differ slightly from the above as a result of changes after the build's release.
