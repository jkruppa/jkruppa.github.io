About

Hi, my name is Jochen Kruppa and I work as Bioinformatican and Biostatistician at the University of Veterinary Medicine Hannover, Foundation (TiHo) in the working group "Genomics and Bioinformatics of Infectious Diseases". I teach quantitative genetics and biostatistics at the Leibniz Universität Hannover. Further, I am also teaching biostatistics at the Borstel Biomedical Research School. For further information please visit my personal department website or write me an email.
